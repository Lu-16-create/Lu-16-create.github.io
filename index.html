繪本組介紹

我們繪本組的指導老師為德國裔國外繪師，Jürgen Eckey，過程中會參與到繪本創作課程，也會與老師課上討論。這次繪本組要做的內容就是要把老師的作品，George-The legend重新畫上插圖，沒有任何限制，自由發揮創意。


故事原文

In times long past there was a city, which was besieged by a dragon. As no other sacrify satisfied it, even the king’s daughter was sent to it. But it didn’t go.

Then George, a brave knight, appeared. He crossed to the island where the dragon lived, and in a hard fight he wounded it so badly, that he was able to take it alive to the city. Impressed by this deed, many citizens converted to Christianity, George’s religion. This displeased the king, who adhered to paganism, and he executed George. Therefor he is called a martyr.

The true story. A parody by Jürgen Eckey I cheated. I am no hero. I admit that I’m rightly punished. But why with death? Can’t they just let me go? It would be enough to deprive me of all these honors, which I don’t live up anyway, and to chase me out of the city in disgrace, where I can no longer show my face!

When I arrived, there was already big fear. A dragon! A dragon! A dragon was up to mischief in front of the city. The hysteria was enormous.

The princess had been offered to the dragon as a “voluntary” sacrifice to put it to rest. Because the king found no help, the anger of the citizens turned against him and he should give his daughter to feed the dragon.

You have to explain to me why a princess tastes different from a middleclass girl? And no dragon gets enough just from a princess! But she had been escorted in a funeral procession to the beach and was crossed over to the island where it had it’s nest. And she was not seen again.

But the evil was not over. The dragon was still there. So a hero was sought to kill it. I just had big problems with some ladies who, not without a matter, asked me to marry them. That’s why I signed up for this impossible mission to get rid of them.

As a former soldier I indeed was able to play the role of the fortified fighter! I let myself be put in a fine armor and was taken to the boat with the sympathy of the whole population. – My plan was to circle the island and escape, unseen by the city.

So why did I end up on the island? Quite simply, because of the princess! I can’t resist any woman. And not only had she survived, she lived peaceful side by side with the dragon!

With her fluteplaying she had calmed and tamed the beast!

Soon we were a couple. She was as passionate as a woman, which lived long time alone, can be. She showed me her garden.

She was so smart!

She had even trained the dragon to hunt for her! With plenty of meat and some fruits and vegetables we had a good food on the island. We could live like in paradise.

But we both were missing human society. So we made a plan for our return: She would be graciously received back at the court of the king and I, as her savior, would be her husband and a high officer in the royal army!

We had a problem with the dragon. It didn’t want to stay alone on his island, but followed the princess like a puppy! No matter how much we shooed it or yelled at it, it could not be driven away. So we let it come along, even though we knew it was not in his interest.

Of course, for both of us the dragon’s presence offered the possibility of a triumphant homecoming!

My princess walked in front, playing the flute, luring the dragon after her. It obediently tripped behind, looking right and left nervously because of all the people. I walked behind the dragon, moving menacing my spear as if I were it’s tamer. No words can describe the jubilation!

The marriage was celebrated in all splendor. But on the wedding feast table there was – dragon tongue! And indeed I was put in a high command of the army. It was like in a fairy tale. But unfortunately I’m not a fairy tale man!

Getting drunken in nice company after weeks of virtuous life as a noble knight – who can describe this feeling! As a dragon slayer, my chances with women were well now and I used them where I could. I even embraced Christianity to seduce a pretty noble lady! Must I mention that I was not able to fulfill my boring military duties?

My little princess was very angry about me. But what she could do – I thought.

She ran to her father, the king, cried to him and he decided to punish me for my new religion. Not for adultery, that is private; but for Christianity as a religion, which doesn’t respect the king, not to cast a shadow on the kings daughter.

Punished to death! This can’t be true! I am waiting for the moment, that the door to my dungeon opens and my princess’ sweet voice whispers: “Hush! George, come!”

完成

整合 後製調色

場景完成後把人物放上去，全部整合。因為每個人畫風都不一樣，所以最後後製調色，達成統一的效果。

草圖

一開始我們都會先把草圖畫出來，確定構圖，方便後續操作淺顯易懂。

場景

知道構圖後會繪製場景，讓之後的角色能放上去。
